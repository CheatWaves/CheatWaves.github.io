Select the weapon for which you will configure Aimbot.

Aimbot

Automatic aiming at the enemy during the shooting.

Engine

Aimbot uses only the game engine, and thanks to this, it becomes more accurate and independent of the sensitivity in the game.

With low FPS it is better to enable this feature.

Enemy only

Aimbot will only work on enemy characters.

Randomize

Turn on a mode that introduces randomness into Aimbot's work.

Distance Based Fov

Turn on the target capture angle mode, which depends on the distance to the target.

FOV

The angle of capture of the target. Aimbot.

Value: 0 to 90. The higher the value, the more targets Aimbot will capture.

Smooth

Aimbot's aiming speed smoothing.

Value: 0 to 50. The larger the value, the slower the Aimbot.

Hitboxes

Part of the body where Aimbot will be pointing.

If several body parts are selected, then aimbot will point to the one closest to your sight.

Delay Type

Aimbot'a delay type.

Breaker - Aimbot does not fully work until the expiration of the specified period.
Waiter - Aimbot aims at the enemy with a delayed shot until the expiration of the specified period.

Delay

Aimbot'a delay time.

Value: 0 to 1000. Measured in milliseconds.

Kill delay

The time that Aimbot disconnects after killing an enemy.

Value: 0 to 1000. Measured in milliseconds.

Auto-pistol

Cartridges from pistols will shoot like an assault rifle (clip).

Jump ignore

Aimbot ignores targets that are in the jump.

Jump check

Aimbot will not work if you are in a jump.

Reload check

Aimbot will not work if you recharge.

Smoke check

Aimbot ignores targets that are inside smoke from a smoke grenade.

Flash check

Aimbot will not work if you are blinded by a light grenade.

Select the weapon for which you will configure the Triggerbot.

Triggerbot

Automatic shot when hovering over a character

Enemy only

Triggerbot will only work on enemy characters.

Only one shot

Triggerbot makes only one shot from a weapon.

Only one shot Delay

Triggerbot shutdown time if Only one shot is enabled and Triggerbot fired one shot

Value: 0 to 1000. Measured in milliseconds.

Active key

The button, when clicked, will trigger the Triggerbot.

Hitboxes

The part of the body that Triggerbot will respond to.

If several body parts are selected, the Triggerbot will respond to the one closest to your sight.

Type

Aimbot'a delay type.

Onkey - Triggerbot works when the action button is pressed
Switch - when you press the action button, the mode is turned on / off when the Triggerbot is constantly running.

Visuals

Enable rendering of visual functions.

Outline

Black stroke of all visual functions.

Enemy only

Visual features will only work on enemy characters.

Visible only

Visual features will only work on visible characters.

Head dot

Show a small dot on the character’s head.

Box

Show character position.

Border - regular rectangle. Corner - only corners from the rectangle.
Centered - square exactly in the center of the character’s body. 3DBox - three-dimensional squares.

Chams

Fill the character with solid color.

Standard - color character with shadow.
Fullbright - color the character without taking into account the shadows.

Name

Show character name.

Weapon

Show weapons that are in the hands of the character.

Text - plain text.
Icon - in the form of an icon.

Health

Show character health.

Text - plain text.
Bar - in the form of a colored strip.

Armor

Show character armor.

Text - plain text.
Bar - in the form of a colored strip.

Skeleton

Show body bones of an enemy character.

Barrel

Show where an enemy or allied character is looking.

Sound

Show the position of sounds made by enemy or allied characters.

Misc

Autostrafe

Automatic strafy when spraying.

Draw Fov

Crosshair

Draw the sight on top of the game.

Dynamic - the sight shows the return of the weapon.
Static - the scope is always in the center of the screen.

Rank

Show character ranks in the results table.

Additional Info

Inform the player if Aimbot or Triggerbot is working

Ingame radar

Enable display of enemy characters on the in-game radar.

Hitinfo

Show information about your shot.

World - information about your shot is displayed at the place of hitting the target.
Screen - information about your shot is displayed at the top of the screen.

Grenade trajectory

Show the trajectory of grenades thrown by you.

No smoke

No flash

Grenade

Show grenades that are in action mode.

Bomb

Show the owner of the bomb, the location of the bomb and the timer before the explosion.

World item

Show the position of objects lying on the map.

Loot

Show the position of collective objects lying on the map in Battle Royale mode.
